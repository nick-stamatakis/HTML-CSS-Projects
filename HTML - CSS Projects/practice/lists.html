How to Make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

This is the main body of my website on how to make sandwiches.
  • 2 slices of sandwich bread
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of jelly
  1. Spread the jelly on one piece of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on the other.
  3. Mush the two pieces together to create the PB and J sandwich.
